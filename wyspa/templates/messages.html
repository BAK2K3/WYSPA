Wyspas

Listen to or comment on a random Wyspa

Each time you visit this page, a random Wyspa will be presented. Refresh the page, click the Wyspas link again, to be shown a new random Wyspa.

To acknowledge you've listened to a Wyspa, interact with the listen icon: hearing. However, you must be logged in to do this. You can listen to your own Wyspa, because if you can't listen to yourself, who can you listen to?

Each Wyspa can only be listened to once per user. For every listen a Wyspa receives, it's marker grows on the map. So the more listens a Wyspa receives, the more it is heard.

This page doesn't display the mood of the Wyspa. This allows users to listen to and acknowledge each other with as little context as possible.

Anyone can comment on Wyspas. For logged in users, you may delete your own comments. Owners of the Wyspa also have the ability to delete comments. Remember that WYSPA doesn't believe in censorship, so it's down to you to respect yourself and each other.
